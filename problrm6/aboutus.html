About Us

Thank you for visiting SportsPageMagazine.com.


Mike D'Avino-Managing editor of SportsPageMagazine

From our start in 2003 as a handful of committed individuals covering local Connecticut sports, our publication has grown – and continues to grow — steadily to a staff of well over 100 writers and photographers nationwide, covering amateur, high school, college and professional sports. If you are a sports fan, you have come to the right place! SportsPageMagazine.com was founded by people just like you — sports fanatics. Our website is updated around the clock as information breaks, often in the form of press releases from every major professional sports league in America and all the conferences of the NCAA. All of this complements the scores of original and feature articles written by our own staff members each week along with the comprehensive photo coverage SportsPageMagazine.com has become known for nationwide. The photo galleries on our website are extraordinary and are viewed by thousands on a daily basis. Please feel free to contact me, Mike D’Avino if you have any questions, comments or would like any further information about our publication. Mike D’Avino SportsPageMagazine.com
